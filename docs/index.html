DDA Algorithm

DDA Algorithm let us now how far is a wall from our player. we shoot laser "Rays" from our player and when it contact the walls, we can use the distance which our Ray traveled and find out where the wall is.

FPS

Textured Walls

from the DDA Algorithm, we can find out which side is the wall facing and with that information, we can texture our with simple colors.
